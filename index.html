OUR STORY

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in the heart of Indonesia's coffee belt, Nuri Coffenesia represents the pinnacle of Indonesian coffee craftsmanship. Our journey began with a simple vision — to bring the world the finest beans from the archipelago's volcanic highlands.

Each bean tells a story of generations of farmers who have perfected the art of cultivation on the fertile slopes. From Sumatra's Mandheling to Java's Preanger, we source only the most exceptional lots, ensuring every cup delivers an unforgettable experience.

Our commitment extends beyond quality — we believe in sustainable farming, fair partnerships, and preserving the cultural heritage that makes Indonesian coffee truly extraordinary.

2018 First plantation established in Sumatra highlands
2019 Expanded sourcing to Java & Sulawesi regions
2020 Launched Nuri Coffenesia brand globally
2022 Received international specialty coffee recognition
2024 Premium product line launched worldwide

OUR STORY

Nature's Finest Gift

Nuri Coconutnesia was born from the lush tropical landscapes of Indonesia, where coconut trees have been a symbol of life and prosperity for centuries. We honor this heritage by delivering the purest coconut products to the world.

Our coconuts are hand-harvested from organic plantations across Java, Bali, and Sulawesi. Each product undergoes meticulous cold-press processing to preserve the natural nutrients and authentic flavor that nature intended.

We are committed to sustainable agriculture, zero-waste production, and empowering the coastal communities that make our mission possible. Every purchase supports a healthier planet and a thriving community.

2018 Organic coconut plantation established in Java
2019 Cold-press facility launched with zero-waste system
2020 Nuri Coconutnesia brand launched internationally
2022 Organic certification & global distribution achieved
2024 Full product line expansion across 30+ countries
